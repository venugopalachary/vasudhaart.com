About Vasudha

Vasudha's life as an artist began when she was in her primary school. Her inspiration as a child came from her mother. Vasudha is an Artist, Therapeutic Art Life Coach, Happiness Life Coach and an Educationist. She has a degree in Commercial Art/ Graphic Designing.
Art chose her by divine intervention. She learnt that art was her salvation, her connection with God,and her love language towards others.

FOUNDER

Either with the gentle stroke of a paint brush, a beautiful puddle of paint, bold fluid art or the glorious depth of mandala, the creation of beauty in art can be extremely therapeutic and inspiring for all.

Vasudha is a multidisciplinary contemporary Indian artist who has explored infinite dimensions within herself and created her own artistic vocabulary. She enjoys multiple styles of art, be it realism, whimsy, pure freedom of an abstract or the surprise of a beautiful mandala/ zentangle art piece.

Vasudha believes in her own magic. Her goal in life is to paint and create artwork that she can share with others, in doing so creating her own happiness. She also transfers this joy and excitement onto canvases, stones, paper and murals. She constantly strives to explore and expand her boundaries and horizons to understand life from the best perspective. The intensity and frequency of her creative output is enormous.
